I am a graduate of Ohio State University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer and Information Science with a specialization in Computer Graphics and Game Design. During high school I took programming classes at a career center and continued my education at Ohio State University with a desire to learn more about game development. I began learning game development with Unreal Engine in 2020 after messing around with OpenGL and Unity for the previous two years. I see game development as a medium that brings together creativity with art into a technical medium which I find to be the most intriguing area of computer science. I have filled my free time with furthering my knowledge in Unreal Engine and hope to release my own game someday for the purpose of realizing my own imaginative ideas.
